Add the Helsinki City bikes widget with a long press on an empty space on the home screen, tapping on the Widgets button and selecting the Helsinki City bikes widget from the list of available widgets (the way to add widgets might be different on your device).

When you add a new bike widget you get to choose which station you want to monitor.

You can add multiple widgets if you want to monitor several stations.

The bike count updates automatically every 30 minutes. Tap one of the bike widgets to update the count on all the widgets immediately.

The station which a widget is monitoring can be changed be removing the widget and adding a new one. When adding a new widget you get to choose the station to monitor.
